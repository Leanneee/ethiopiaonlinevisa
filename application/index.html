Ethiopia eVisa Application

Apply now for eVisa

The online Ethiopia Visa application has been designed to be straightforward and quick to complete. The secure online form only takes a matter of minutes to complete and applications are normally processed within 24 hours.

How to Obtain a Visa for Ethiopa Online

The eVisa is an online tourist visa application that saves applicants wishing to travel to Ethiopia time and effort. Applying for an online visa for Ethiopia is completely safe and does not require visiting the embassy or consulate. The online process is very easy to complete and allows tourists to have an approved eVISA ready before departing on their trip.

To obtain an Ethiopian eVisa, passport holders of eligible countries will be required to complete a simple online application form. Applicants will need to enter their personal details such as their full name, date of birth and street address. They then will be required to disclose information about their trip, as well as answer some security-related questions.

In order to apply for an online Ethiopian eVisa, applicants will need to have the following on hand:

  • Valid passport
  • A credit/debit card
  • Valid email address

Every section of the Ethiopia Visa application will need to be answered as accurately as possible. You are advised to double check for typing errors in your email address as well. Incorrect or missing information could result in delays, or the application being rejected. Once the travel authorization has been approved, it will be sent to the applicant via email.

Filling out the Ethiopian Visa Application Form


The online Ethiopia visa registration is simple to fill out. Applicants just need to answer the questions with the required information which include the applicant’s full name, street address, date of birth, email address, and travel plans.

Applicants must also enter their passport details accurately and answer some health and security-related questions. In total, the process of filling out the form does not takes longer than a few minutes in most cases.

You are advised to make sure that every section of the Ethiopia Visa application is completed  as precisely as possible. Please remember that any incorrect or missing data could result in delays, or in denial of the application . Once the travel authorization has been granted, it will be sent securely to the applicant via email.

Reviewing Information and Confirming Payment


To successfully submit the Ethiopia Online Visa application it is necessary to meet the visa requirements and pay a fee using a credit or debit card. Applicants need to ensure that every section of the Ethiopia eVisa application is completed accurately. Any inaccuracies or incomplete sections could lead to the application being delayed.

Applicants must have a valid passport with at least six months remaining prior to its expiry date at the moment they wish to travel to Ethiopia. Please note that the passport used to apply for the Ethiopian visa must be the same as the one used to enter the country.

Ethiopia eVisa Processing Time

After the Ethiopian visa application form has been submitted the information provided is duly screened by the Ethiopian authorities. The vast majority of applications will be processed and approved within 24 hours.

In rare cases, the Ethiopia visa application process can take up to 72 hours to process. To minimize the chances of delays, applicants are advised to ensure that all the information is entered accurately and that no sections are left incomplete.

Receiving the Approved Ethiopian eVisa


The email address provided on the secure application form will be used to deliver the eVisa. Once approved, the Ethiopia eVisa will be sent electronically over secure servers to the applicant by email. It is necessary to print a copy to present to immigration authorities at the border in order to gain entry into Ethiopia upon your arrival. Travelers should make sure that the printed copy is clear and legible.

The email address provided on the application form will be used to deliver the eVisa. The Ethiopia eVisa allows visitors to enter for either 30 days or 90 days depending on the selection made on the application form.
