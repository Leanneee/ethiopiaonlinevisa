Ethiopian Embassies around the World

Ethiopia has 43 embassies and 49 consulates around the world. There are a number of reasons why you might need to go to an embassy, there is a range of processes and applications which can only be completed there.

However, for tourists wanting to visit Ethiopia, there is no need to go to an embassy or consulate. The Ethiopian Government has made the Ethiopia eVisa available to all nationalities.

The online eVisa application only takes a few minutes to complete. There is are 30-day visas and 90-day visas available.
